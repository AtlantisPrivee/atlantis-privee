Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Can you provide legal advice for my case?

No. Atlantis Privée is NOT a law firm and we do NOT provide legal advice. Our content is for informational and educational purposes only. For specific legal questions, please consult with a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction. See our Disclaimer for more information.

Can you recommend a specific attorney?

We don't make individual attorney recommendations to maintain our editorial independence. We suggest contacting your state bar association for referrals to qualified attorneys in your area.

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Partnership inquiries and complex requests may take 3-5 business days.
