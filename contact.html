Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

?

Can you provide legal advice for my case?

No. Atlantis Privée is NOT a law firm and we do NOT provide legal advice. Our content is for informational and educational purposes only. For specific legal questions, please consult with a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction. See our Disclaimer for more information.

?

Can you recommend a specific attorney?

We don't make individual attorney recommendations to maintain our editorial independence. We suggest contacting your state bar association for referrals to qualified attorneys in your area, or using established legal directories like Avvo, FindLaw, or Martindale-Hubbell.

?

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Partnership inquiries and complex requests may take 3-5 business days. We read every message and try to respond personally to all serious inquiries.

?

Do you accept guest posts?

We occasionally accept high-quality guest posts from qualified contributors with expertise in personal injury law, insurance, or related fields. Please contact us with your proposal, credentials, and writing samples. Sponsored posts must be clearly disclosed.

?

Can I use your content on my website?

All content on Atlantis Privée is copyrighted. You may not republish our content without express written permission. However, you may share excerpts (under 200 words) with proper attribution and a link back to the original article. Please contact us for licensing or republication requests. See our Terms of Service for more details.

?

I found an error in an article. How do I report it?

We appreciate your help in maintaining accuracy! Please use the form above or email us at contact@atlantisprivee.com with the subject "Correction Request". Include the article URL and the specific information you believe needs correction. We review all correction requests promptly.

?

Do you offer advertising opportunities?

Yes, we offer various advertising options including display ads, sponsored content, and partnership opportunities. Please contact us with your business name, target audience, and campaign goals for a customized proposal.

?

Can I unsubscribe from your newsletter?

Absolutely. Every newsletter we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can also email us at contact@atlantisprivee.com to be removed from our mailing list. We respect your privacy preferences completely.
