Frequently Asked Questions

Can you provide legal advice for my case?

No. Atlantis Privée is NOT a law firm and we do NOT provide legal advice. For specific legal questions, please consult with a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction. See our Disclaimer for more information.

Can you recommend a specific attorney?

We don't make individual attorney recommendations. We suggest contacting your state bar association for referrals to qualified attorneys in your area.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Complex inquiries may take longer.

Do you accept guest posts?

We occasionally accept high-quality guest posts from qualified contributors. Please contact us with your proposal and credentials.

Can I use your content on my website?
