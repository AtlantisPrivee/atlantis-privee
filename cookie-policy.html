About Cookies on Our Site

This Cookie Policy explains how Atlantis Privée uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control them.

🍪
Section 01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Types of Cookies

  • First-party cookies: Set by the website you are visiting
  • Third-party cookies: Set by other websites whose services are used on the visited website
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

Section 02

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the Site.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the Site
  • How visitors arrived at our Site
  • Device and browser information

Advertising Cookies

We use cookies from Google AdSense and other advertising networks to display relevant advertisements:

  • Remember that you have visited our Site
  • Display ads relevant to your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see the same ad
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality, such as remembering your preferences and choices (like cookie consent).

Section 05

How to Control Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites
  • Get notified when cookies are being set

Cookie Management by Browser

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out of Specific Services

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads: Adjust your Google Ads Settings
  • Advertising Network: Visit DAA Opt-Out Page
🍪

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Site, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies or customize your preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

Section 06

Impact of Disabling Cookies

⚠️

Please Note

If you disable or refuse cookies, certain features of our Site may not work properly.

Specifically, disabling cookies may result in:

Do Not Track Signals
